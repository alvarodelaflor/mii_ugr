Seminar on WS-CDL

The Services Choreography Description Language (WS-CDL) is an XML-based language 

that describes peer-to-peer collaborations of participants by defining, from a 

global viewpoint, their common and complementary observable behavior; where 

ordered message exchanges result in accomplishing a common business goal'',
