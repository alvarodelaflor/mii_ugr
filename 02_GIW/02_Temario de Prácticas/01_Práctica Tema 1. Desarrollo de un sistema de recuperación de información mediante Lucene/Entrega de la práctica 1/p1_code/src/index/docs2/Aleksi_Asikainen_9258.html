Aleksi Asikainen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aleksi Asikainen is the president of media services company Infire Ltd. and co-creator and lead programmer of the online game Furcadia.[1] He is also a music composer associated primarily with Finnish television.[2]

Since 2001, Infire Business Ventures, a subsidiary of Infire Ltd., has managed software development, server administration, and online sales for Furcadia.
