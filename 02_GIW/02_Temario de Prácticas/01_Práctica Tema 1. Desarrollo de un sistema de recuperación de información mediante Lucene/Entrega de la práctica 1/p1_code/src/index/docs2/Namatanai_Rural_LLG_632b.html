Namatanai Rural LLG

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of The Namatanai Rural LLG in the Namatanai District of New Ireland Province in Papua New Guinea

The Namatanai Rural LLG is a local government area in New Ireland Province, Papua New Guinea. The LLG is located in Namatanai District and the LLG headquarters is Namatanai.

The LLG is accessible by road from Kavieng and is bordered by Sentral Niu Ailan Rural LLG in the north and Konoagil Rural LLG in the south.

The current LLG president is Alfred Elisha.
