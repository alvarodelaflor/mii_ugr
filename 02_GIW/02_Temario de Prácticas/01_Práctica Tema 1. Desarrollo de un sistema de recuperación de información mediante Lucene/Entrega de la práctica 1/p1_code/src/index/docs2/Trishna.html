Trishna

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Trishna can mean:

  • Tanha, the Buddhist concept of thirst or desire
  • Trishna (polish mythology), the goddess of the deceased
  • Trishna (TV series), a famous TV serial in India in the 90s, which was an adaptation of the novel Pride and Prejudice
  • Trishna (name), a common Hindu/Sanskrit name meaning thirst, or desire, or a form of the devi
  • Trishna (Parkour Clan), a group of seven "traceurs" (practisioners of parkour) based in/around Belfast, the seven members nicknamed (alphabetically) Air-Bourne, Fulcrum, Insidious, Niro, Raziel, Remus and Tauro

In regards to sailing, Trishna can mean:

  • Trishna (expedition), the first Indian sailing expedition around the world
  • TRISHNA, the ship used on that expedition
  • Trishna (book), a book about the event by Don Moraes.
