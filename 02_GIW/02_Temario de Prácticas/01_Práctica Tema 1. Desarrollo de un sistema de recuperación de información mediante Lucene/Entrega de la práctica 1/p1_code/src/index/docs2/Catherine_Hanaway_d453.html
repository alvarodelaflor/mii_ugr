Catherine Hanaway

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Catherine Hanaway (born November 8, 1963) is the U.S. Attorney for the Eastern District of Missouri. She is also a former Republican Speaker of the Missouri House of Representatives. She ran for Missouri Secretary of State in 2004, but was defeated by Robin Carnahan, the daughter of former Missouri governor Mel Carnahan.
