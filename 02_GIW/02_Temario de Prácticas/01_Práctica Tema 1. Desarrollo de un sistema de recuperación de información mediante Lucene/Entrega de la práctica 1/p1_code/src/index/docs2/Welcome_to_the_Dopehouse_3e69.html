From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Welcome to the Dopehouse
Studio album by The Dayton Family
Released 2002
Label Koch records

Welcome to the Dopehouse is the 3rd album recorded by Flint, Michigan native rap group The Dayton Family. It was originally released in 2002 under Koch records.

  8. Drugstore
  9. Set Up

  11. Dope House
  12. Young Thugs (featuring Ghetto E & Lori)
  13. Big Mac 11
  14. Weed Song
  15. Simple Wish
  16. Outro
