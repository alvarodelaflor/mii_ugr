Seobinggo Station is a station on the Yongsan-Deokso Line. It is located near the northern end of Banpo Bridge. The southeastern part of the Yongsan Garrison, a military base in Seoul, is served by this station. The Embassy of Turkey is close from Exit 1.

[edit] Overview

Sindongah(신동아) apartment complex is just near the station. CGN TV broadcasting office and Onnuri community church are also located here. Hank Bank and shopping malls are available. If you need to get buses, NO.0015 /0211 /6211 are available.[1]


[edit] Vicinity

  • Exit 1 : Seobinggo Elementary School, Turkish Embassy of Korea, Hangang Middle School
  • Exit 2 : Sindonga APT, Banpo Bridge, Jamsu Bridge
