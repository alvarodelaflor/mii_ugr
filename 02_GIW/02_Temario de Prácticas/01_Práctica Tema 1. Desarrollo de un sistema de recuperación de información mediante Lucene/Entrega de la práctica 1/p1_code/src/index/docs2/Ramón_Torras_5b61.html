Ramón Torras (December 22, 1942 in Barcelona - May 30, 1965 in Comarruga) was a former Grand Prix motorcycle road racer from Spain. His best year was 1965, when he finished in eighth place in the 250cc world championship. In 1965, Torras was killed at a race in Comarruga, Spain.
