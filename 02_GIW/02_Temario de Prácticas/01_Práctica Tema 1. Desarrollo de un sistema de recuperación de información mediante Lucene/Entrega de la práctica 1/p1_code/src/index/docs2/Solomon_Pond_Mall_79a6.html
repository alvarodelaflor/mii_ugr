Solomon Pond Mall

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Solomon Pond Mall is operated and co-owned by Simon Property Group and is located off Interstate 290, near its terminus at Interstate 495, in Marlborough, Massachusetts. The 900,000 square foot mall opened in the summer of 1996. The mall includes a big carousel for children located in the food court. (carousel gone as of 6/9/08)

Recent additions to the mall include Coach, Lucky Brand Jeans, and Ann Taylor Loft.
