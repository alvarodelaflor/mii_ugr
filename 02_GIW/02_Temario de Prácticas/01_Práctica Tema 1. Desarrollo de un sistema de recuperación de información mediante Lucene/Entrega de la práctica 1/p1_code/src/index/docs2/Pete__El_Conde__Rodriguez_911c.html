Pete "El Conde" Rodriguez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pedro Juan Rodriguez Ferrer (January 31, 1933 – December 2, 2000), better known as Pete "El Conde" Rodriguez was a salsa singer born in Ponce, Puerto Rico.

He also had a son named Pete Rodriguez who is also a salsa and jazz musician.[1]

[edit] Discography

  • El Conde (1974)
  • Fiesta Con "El Conde (1982)
  • El Rey (1990)
  • Generaciones (1993)
  • Yo Vengo Soltando Chispas Hot & Wild (1999)
  • A Touch of Class (1999)
  • Pete & Papo (1999)
  • Este Negro Si Es Sabroso (2006)
