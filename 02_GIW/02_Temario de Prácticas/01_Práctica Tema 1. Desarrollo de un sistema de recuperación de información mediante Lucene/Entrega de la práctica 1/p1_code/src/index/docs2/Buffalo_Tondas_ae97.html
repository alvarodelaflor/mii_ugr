Buffalo Tondas

Buffalo Tondas
City: Buffalo, New York
League: Southern Ontario Junior A Hockey League
Operated: 1973-74
Colors: Blue, Red, and White

The Buffalo Tondas are a defunct Tier II Junior "A" ice hockey team that played out of Buffalo, New York. They played out of the Southern Ontario Junior A Hockey League. Possibly became the Buffalo Jr. Sabres.
