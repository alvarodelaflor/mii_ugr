Ulrich Karger

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ulrich Karger (* February 3, 1957 in Berchtesgaden Bavaria Germany) is an author and teacher at a school in Berlin.

Ulrich Karger

His publications are aimed at children and adults. The complete prose retelling of Homers Odyssey as a book for young people which much also received laudatory words of encouragement from the criticism in the complete German linguistic area is one of his most successful works. Beside other several languages is his picture book for children Geisterstunde im Kindergarten being published in English as The Scary Sleepover. In addition, for years he has been writing also many book reviews for various daily papers and programme magazines.

He established the freely accessible online review archives Buechernachlese[1] in 1999. Under this have to be called over 1,400 of his book reviews and short indications for fiction and poetry, non-fiction book as well as children's books and literature for young people.
