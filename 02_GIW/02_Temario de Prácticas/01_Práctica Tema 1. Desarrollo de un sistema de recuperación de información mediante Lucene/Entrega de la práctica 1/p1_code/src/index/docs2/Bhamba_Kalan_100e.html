Bhamba Kalan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bhamba Kalan is a town and Union Council of Kasur District in the Punjab province of Pakistan.[1] It is part of Kasur Tehsil and is located at 31°11'21N 74°12'8E with an altitude of 203 metres (669 feet).[2]
