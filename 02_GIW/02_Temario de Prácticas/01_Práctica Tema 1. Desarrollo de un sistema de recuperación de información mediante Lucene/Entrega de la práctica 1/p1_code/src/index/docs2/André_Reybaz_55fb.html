André Reybaz

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andre Reybaz (born 29 October 1929 in Paris, France) is a French actor. Died in 1989.

Reybaz had a long career spanning forty years, which mostly consisted of numerous French television appearances. In 1950 he starred in the writer Jean Genet's only film, the tremendously influential Un chant d'amour (aka A Song Of Love), which focused on the longing desires of the guard and prisoners of a French jail.
