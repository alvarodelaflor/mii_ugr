Rotary Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium located in Abbotsford, British Columbia. It is the home of the Abbotsford Air Force of the Canadian Junior Football League. Besides hosting field events, it has a rubberized 400m running track ("Jane and Gerry Swan Track") and facilities for other track and field events. The stadium seats 4,000.

Rotary Stadium is part of Abbotsford Exhibition Park, a 75 acre park located in the heart of Abbotsford. The park will be undergoing a revitalization project based on the Exhibition Park Master Plan.

The stadium has been used for BC Lions training camp activities.
