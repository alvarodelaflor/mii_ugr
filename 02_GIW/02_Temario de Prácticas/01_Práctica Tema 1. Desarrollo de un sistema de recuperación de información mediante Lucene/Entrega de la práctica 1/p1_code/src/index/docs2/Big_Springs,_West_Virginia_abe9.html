Big Springs, West Virginia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Big Springs (also Bigsprings, Chestnut Grove, or Fort Evans) is an unincorporated community in Calhoun County, West Virginia, United States. It lies along West Virginia Route 16 to the north of the town of Grantsville, the county seat of Calhoun County.[1] Its elevation is 771 feet (235 m), and it is located at 38°39′23″N, 81°4′28″W (38.6564838, -81.0745550).[2] Although it is unincorporated, it has a post office, with the ZIP code of 26137.[3]
