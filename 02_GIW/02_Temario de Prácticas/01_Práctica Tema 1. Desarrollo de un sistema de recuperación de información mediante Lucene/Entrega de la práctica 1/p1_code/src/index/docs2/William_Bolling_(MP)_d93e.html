William Bolling (MP)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other persons named William Bolling, see William Bolling (disambiguation).

William Bolling (1785 – 30 August 1848) was a British politician. He was the Tory Member of Parliament (MP) for Bolton from 1832 to 1841, and Conservative MP for Bolton from 1847 until his death in 1848.
