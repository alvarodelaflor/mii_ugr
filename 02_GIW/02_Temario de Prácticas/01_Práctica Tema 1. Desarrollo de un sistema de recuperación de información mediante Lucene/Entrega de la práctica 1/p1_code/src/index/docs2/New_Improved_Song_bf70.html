New Improved Song

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

“New Improved Song”
Song by Faith No More
Album Sounds Waves 2
Released 1988
Recorded 1988
Genre Alternative metal
Length 3:45
Label Slash
Producer Matt Wallace

New Improved Song was the first song released after Faith No More's second full-length, Introduce Yourself, and the last to feature Chuck Mosely on vocals. It is an early version of "The Morning After", and was later re-recorded for The Real Thing. It was only released on the second issue of Sounds, a music magazine of the 1980s.

Like most Faith No More demos, it was recorded on an 8-track by Matt Wallace. Other songs to have surfaced from 8-track recordings are "Introduce Yourself", "Greed" and "The Jungle".
