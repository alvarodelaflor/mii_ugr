Ursula Mayes (born August 9, 1979) is an American model and television host.

[edit] Early life

Ursula Mayes was born on August 9, in a military base in Seoul, South Korea. Born to a Korean Mother and a German/ Native-American Father.

[edit] Modeling career

Having traveled so much as a child, now carries on into her current life. Landing a spread in Maxim Magazine and shortly after, a spokesmodel campaign with a global events company she embarked on a 20-city nation wide tour. Currently, she is their global spokesmodel with travels extending to Asia, Europe, and Guam. Ursula is also the host for Action Media interviewing various celebrities, musicians, athletes and corporate sponsors.

On primetime television, Ursula appears as suitcase model #5 on NBC's hit game show Deal or No Deal. Ursula is currently pursuing her acting career and taking scripts for lead roles.
