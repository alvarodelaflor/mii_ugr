Achsah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This entry incorporates text from the public domain Easton's Bible Dictionary, originally published in 1897.


Achsah - "anklet", was Caleb ben Yefune's only daughter (1 Chr. 2:49). She was offered in marriage to the man who would lead an attack on the city of Debir, also called Kirjath-sepher/Kirjath-sannah. This was done by Othniel (q.v.), Caleb's younger brother, who accordingly obtained her as his wife (Joshua 15:16-19; Judges 1:9-15).

Achsah later requests, and is given, upper and lower springs of water (presumably in the Negev) from her father. (Judges 1:14-15)
