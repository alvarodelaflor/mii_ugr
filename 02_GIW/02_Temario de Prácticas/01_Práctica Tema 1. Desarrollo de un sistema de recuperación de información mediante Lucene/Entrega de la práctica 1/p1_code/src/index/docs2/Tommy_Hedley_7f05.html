Tommy Hedley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tommy Hedley (died 8 August 2007) was a businessman and the chairman of the semi-professional football club Blyth Spartans F.C. in Blyth, Northumberland He died of cancer in 2007.[1]
