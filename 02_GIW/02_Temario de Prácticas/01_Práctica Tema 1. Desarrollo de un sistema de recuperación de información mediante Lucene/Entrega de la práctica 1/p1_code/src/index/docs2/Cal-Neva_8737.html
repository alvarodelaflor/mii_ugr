Cal-Neva

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cal-Neva is a lodge and casino located in Lake Tahoe, California. It first opened in 1926, and currently has a total of 220 rooms consisting of a variety of rooms including Cabins, Chalets and Tower Rooms.[1] This lodge was once part-owned by Frank Sinatra.
