Veerakeralam is a panchayat town in Coimbatore district in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India census[1], Veerakeralam had a population of 19,993. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Veerakeralam has an average literacy rate of 77%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 82%, and female literacy is 72%. In Veerakeralam, 9% of the population is under 6 years of age.
