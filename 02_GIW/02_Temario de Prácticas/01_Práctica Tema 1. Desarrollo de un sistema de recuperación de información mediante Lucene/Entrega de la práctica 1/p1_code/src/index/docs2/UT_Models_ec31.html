UT Models

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

UT was a company which manufactured 1:18 scale model cars in the 80's and 90's. They were typically of good to high quality and were a bit more expensive than the competitors. The company is now out of business but was turned into AUTOart models. Famous UT models include the Porsche 993 series as well as the McLaren F1 series. The most rare and hard to find UT models are the street version of the Porsche 993 GT2R in white (which go for upwards of $300) and the silver and dark blue versions of the McLaren F1 LM, which can each go for upwards of $400.
