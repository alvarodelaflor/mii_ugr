Red dragon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Red dragon may refer to the following:

  • Red Dragon, a novel by Thomas Harris
  • Manhunter (film), a 1986 film based on the novel Red Dragon by Thomas Harris
  • Red Dragon (film), a 2002 film based on the novel by Thomas Harris (a remake of Manhunter)
  • The Great Red Dragon Paintings, a series of paintings by the English poet and painter William Blake, one of which figures in largely with the plot of the Thomas Harris novel
  • Red dragon (Dungeons & Dragons), a powerful adversary in the Dungeons & Dragons roleplaying game
  • Red Dragon Syndicate a group that specializes in crime in Cowboy Bebop
  • Legend of the Red Dragon, a bulletin board system door game
  • Red Dragon Society, a secret society at New York University
  • A mahjong tile with the Chinese character for "center" on it
  • The Red Dragon clan in Mortal Kombat
  • Compilers: Principles, Techniques, and Tools by Aho, Ravi, and Ullman, a computer science textbook commonly known as the Red Dragon book due to the red dragon on the cover
  • The Grand Grimoire, a grimoire of magic, is also known as The Red Dragon

[edit] In Welsh culture

The red dragon (Welsh: Y Ddraig Goch) is a national symbol of Wales. Thus, "red dragon" in Welsh culture may refer to:

  • The flag of Wales
  • Red Dragon FM, a radio station serving Cardiff and Newport, Wales
  • Red Dragon (cheese), a Welsh cheese
