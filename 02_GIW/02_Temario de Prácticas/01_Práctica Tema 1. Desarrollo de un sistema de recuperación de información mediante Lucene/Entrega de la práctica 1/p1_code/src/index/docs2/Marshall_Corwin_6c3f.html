Marshall Corwin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Marshall Corwin is a British television producer.

He is currently the producer of the BBC Children's television Serious

To date he has produced Serious Jungle (2002), Serious Desert(2003), Serious Arctic(2004), Serious Amazon (2005) and Serious Andes (2006), which will go to air in late 2007. A new documentary, Serious Ocean, is currently in production (2007). Marshall is also the author of a book to accompany the Serious adventures: 'How to Poo in the Arctic. Forward by Bruce Parry'. [[Image:how to poo in the arctic

In addition he has written or co-written several other children's factual books.

.jpg]]

Serious is a documentary series made by the BBC and broadcast as part of its children's programming.

Marshall Corwin was formerly a producer on the children's news programme Newsround, with responsibility for its short documentaries, Newsound Extra. He has also been a producer in BBC factual programmes. He was also a producer for "Tomorrow's World".

He is currently living in London with his partner and two children.
