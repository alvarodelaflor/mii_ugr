Second banana

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A second banana is a performer who serves as a comic foil opposing a comedian in a double act.[citation needed]

The term derives from burlesque, around 1930,[citation needed] where the slang term "banana" referred to a comedian.[citation needed]

In the I Love Lucy show, Lucille Ball was the top banana, and Vivian Vance was the second banana.
