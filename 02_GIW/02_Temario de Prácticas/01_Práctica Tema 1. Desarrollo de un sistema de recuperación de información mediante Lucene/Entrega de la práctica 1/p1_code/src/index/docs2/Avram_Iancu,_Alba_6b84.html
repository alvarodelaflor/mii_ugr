Avram Iancu, Alba

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Avram Iancu (Hungarian: Felsővidra) is a commune located in Alba County, Romania. Originally called Vidra de Sus, it was renamed after Avram Iancu, a national hero of Romania, which was born here. The principal touristic attraction are the Avram Iancu Memorial House and the extensive network of pitoresque alpine guesthouses maintained by the local inhabitants. The area is famous for the pitoresque scenic alpine landscapes and for organic gourmet foods. The main economic activities in the community are: agriculture (mainly animal husbandry), logging and recently, the fast growing alpine agro-tourism attracting people from all over Europe.

Its mayor as of 2006 is Sandu Heler.
