Tigris Secondary School for Girls

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Tigris Secondary School for Girls is a prestigious convent-style girls' school in the Alawaya district of Baghdad, Iraq. It has some 900 students aged 13-17.

After the 2003 Invasion of Iraq . Former headmistress Sister Clara, who had been removed by the Iraqi Ministry of Education in 1999, due to her liability in (al-tadrees al-Khusosi) which was forbidden in the educational system in Iraq before the 2003 Invasion of Iraq, was able to return to her post.
