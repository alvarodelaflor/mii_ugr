Supermalt

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Supermalt is a malt energy drink rich in B-vitamin complexes. It is a non-alcoholic dark brown drink. It can be purchased in both 330-mL bottles and cans, and their respective six-packs.

Its popularity[citation needed] has led it to be available in a ginger-flavoured variety[citation needed]. It can be bought in large retail outlets such as Asda and Tesco, in corner shops, and in designated supermalt stores, mainly to be found in multicultural areas of major cities.

Supermalt is produced by Royal Unibrew in Denmark. They have also released Supermalt Plus, with added ginseng and aloe vera. It's also very popular in the Netherlands and Nigeria.

Supermalt has a website at Supermalt.com
