Founded 2002
Manager(s) Dave Rotten
Distributing label Many
Genre(s) Death metal, Black metal
Country Spain
Web address http://www.xtreemmusic.com/

Xtreem Music is an independent Spanish record label, founded after the collapse of Repulse Records in 2002, and managed by Dave Rotten (vocalist of Avulsed). It mainly produce extreme death metal and black metal bands.
