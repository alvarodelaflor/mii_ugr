The Journey Continues is an album by Australian musician Tommy Emmanuel. It is a double CD release and contains the original Australian release of The Journey plus nine additional songs.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Tailin' The Invisible Man"
  2. "Big Brother"
  3. "Somethin's Goin' On"
  4. "Hellos & Goodbyes"
  5. "The Journey"
  6. "If Your Heart Tells You To"
  7. "Like Family"
  8. "Don't Hold Me Back"
  9. "Villa Anita"
  10. "White Picket Fences"
  11. "The Big Swell"
  12. "Amy"

Additional tracks:

  1. "Don't Argue"
  2. "Since We Met"
  3. "The Hunt"
  4. "Michelle" (Lennon/McCartney)
  5. "Day Tripper/Lady Madonna" (Lennon/McCartney)
  6. "Fear of Rain"
  7. "Keep It Simple"
  8. "Sunset"
  9. "Southern Summers"

[edit] Personnel

  • Tommy Emmanuel - guitar, bass, percussion
  • Chet Atkins - guitar
  • Jerry Goodman - violin
  • Abraham Laboriel - bass
  • Doanne Perry - drums
  • Rick Neigher - keyboards
  • Kevin Savigar - musette
  • Carlos Vega - drums
  • Joe Walsh - guitar, slide guitar
