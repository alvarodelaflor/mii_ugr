Stanisław Potocki (1734-1802) was a Polish nobleman (szlachcic).

Stanisław was Great Krajczy of the Crown from 1776 to 1780, Rotmistrz of a Husar Choragiew, starost of Lezajsk and Knight of the Order of the White Eagle, awarded in 1769.
