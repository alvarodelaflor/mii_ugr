Teledyne Brown Engineering, or TBE, formerly Brown Engineering, is a Huntsville, ALâ€“based subsidiary of Teledyne Technologies (NYSE: TDY). TBE was acquired by Teledyne Technologies in 1966. TBE provides software engineering and technology services to many clients, including the United States government and NASA (including support for the International Space Station).

TBE includes the wholly-owned subsidiaries:

  • Teledyne Solutions, Inc. (aka TSI), centered in Huntsville
  • Teledyne Brown CollaborX, Inc. (aka TBC)
