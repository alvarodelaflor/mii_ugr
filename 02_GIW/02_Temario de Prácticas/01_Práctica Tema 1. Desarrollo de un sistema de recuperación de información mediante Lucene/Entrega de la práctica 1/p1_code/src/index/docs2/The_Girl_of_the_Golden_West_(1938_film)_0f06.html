The Girl of the Golden West is a 1938 musical western film. It was adapted from the play of the same name by David Belasco, which was in turn based on the opera La fanciulla del West by Giacomo Puccini. A frontier woman falls in love with an outlaw.

  • Leonard Penn as Pedro

  • Cliff Edwards as Minstrel Joe
