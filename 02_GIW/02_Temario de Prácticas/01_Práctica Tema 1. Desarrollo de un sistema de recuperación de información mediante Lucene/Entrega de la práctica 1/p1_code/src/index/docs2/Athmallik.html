Athmallik is a town and a notified area committee in Anugul district in the state of Orissa, India.

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India census,[1] Athmallik had a population of 11,383. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Athmallik has an average literacy rate of 65%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 59% of the males and 41% of females literate. 13% of the population is under 6 years of age.
