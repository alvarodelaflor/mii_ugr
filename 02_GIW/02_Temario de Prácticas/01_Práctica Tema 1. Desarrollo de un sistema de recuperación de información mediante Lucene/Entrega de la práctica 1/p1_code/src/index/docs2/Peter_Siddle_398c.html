Peter Matthew Siddle (born November 25, 1984 in Traralgon) is an Australian cricketer who is a member of the Victorian Bushrangers side. He is a right-arm medium-fast bowler and once took 11 for 47 in a state under-17 match which broke the state record set by John Scholes. He is a former member of Latrobe Cricket Club in Gippsland. Over the last two years, Siddle has become one of the preferred pace bowlers in the Bushrangers squad and has been touted by some as a possible future member of the national team.

Peter Siddle plays for CV Premier Grade team Dandenong. He played a pivotal role in Dandenongs first Premiership in Premier Cricket.
