The Ultimate Guide to Anal Sex for Women

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Ultimate Guide to Anal Sex for Women is a book authored by Tristan Taormino. The first edition of the book came out in 1997, and the second edition was released in 2006. This book was the winner of a Firecracker Book Award and was also named Amazon.com's #1 Bestseller in Women's Sex Instruction in 1998. In 1999 Taormino co-directed a movie based on the book that was titled Tristan Taormino's Ultimate Guide to Anal Sex for Women. The video was released by Evil Angel and co-directed by John Stagliano and Ernest Greene, with Tristan leading the workshop and performing in the orgy at the end of the video. The second video, titled Tristan Taormino's Ultimate Guide to Anal Sex for Women #2, was released in 2000. The second movie featured one-on-one instruction from Tristan for each scene, a Pegging scene, and different actors. Both movies received high critical acclaim and the first won two AVN Awards.
