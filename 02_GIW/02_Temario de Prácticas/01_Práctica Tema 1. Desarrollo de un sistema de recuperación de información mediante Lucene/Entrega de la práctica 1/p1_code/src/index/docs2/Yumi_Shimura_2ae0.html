Yumi Shimura

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Yumi Shimura (志村由美, Shimura Yumi?) was born on November 30, 1982 in Yamanashi, Japan. She works as a seiyū (voice actress) for Japanese anime. She is employed by the talent management firm I'm Enterprise.

[edit] Roles

  • Chisame Hasegawa - Mahou Sensei Negima!
  • Kanaria - Rozen Maiden Träumend
  • Chisame Hasegawa - Negima!?

[edit] Video games

  • Arcana Heart - Saki Tsuzura
  • Ar tonelico II (Kokona Vateel)
