Matt Thompson (animator)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Matt Thompson is an American voice actor, writer, director and producer. He is most often associated with Adult Swim and 70/30 Productions.

He has been a voice actor, writer, director and producer for Sealab 2021 and Frisky Dingo, which he co-created with Adam Reed. He has also voiced character "Producer Man" in 12 oz. Mouse.
