Srisuriyendra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Queen Srisuriyendra (1767-1836, Thai: สมเด็จพระศรีสุริเยนทราบรมราชินี) was the queen consort of King Rama II of Thailand. The daughter of Princess Sri Sudaraksa and her Chinese husband,[1] her given name is Princess Bunrod. She was the mother of King Mongkut (Rama IV) and Vice-king Pinklao. King Mongkut promoted his mother to be Queen Mother Srisuriyendramataya (สมเด็จพระศรีสุริเยนทรามาตย์).
