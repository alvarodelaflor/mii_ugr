Auliya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the religious figure from the Dune universe, see Zensunni

Auliya (awlia, aaliya, aliya) is an Arabic word usually translated as friend, helper, supporter or protector[1]. It's often used to designate the status of a saint. For example, the famous Nizamuddin Auliya became known as such after his religious contributions. One of his sayings also demonstrates the use of the word Auliya to designate saintlihood: "The love of Awlia (saints) is stronger than their reason."
