Vinland Saga is the second full-length album of the symphonic metal band Leaves' Eyes, released in 2005. It is a concept album that tells the story of the voyage of Leif Erikson and his discovery of Vinland (a.k.a. Newfoundland). The main vocals are by Liv Kristine, but there are some death growls by Alexander Krull.
