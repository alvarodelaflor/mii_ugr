Valeriy Yakuzhin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Medal record
Luge
World Championships
Silver 1978 Imst Men's doubles

Valeriy Yakuzhin was a Soviet luger who competed during the late 1970s. He won the silver medal at the men's doubles event at the 1978 FIL World Luge Championships in Imst, Austria. [1]
