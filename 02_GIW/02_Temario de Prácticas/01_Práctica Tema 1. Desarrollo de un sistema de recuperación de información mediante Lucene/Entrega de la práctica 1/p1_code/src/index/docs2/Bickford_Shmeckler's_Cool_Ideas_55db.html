Bickford Shmeckler's Cool Ideas is a 2006 film directed by Scott Lew, starring Patrick Fugit and Olivia Wilde.

[edit] Plot

Bickford Shmeckler (Patrick Fugit) is a lonely college student who keeps a journal of his ideas. During a party, his book is stolen by Sarah Witt (Olivia Wilde), which sends Bickford on a hunt around campus to find his book. Meanwhile, others manage to read the book and fall in love with it.
