Ron Gans

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ronald "Ron" Gans is an American voice-over artist and character actor, known for portraying "Q. T." the orangatan on The Disney Channel's Dumbo's Circus and the voice of Eeyore on Welcome to Pooh Corner.
