"The Man of Steel Bars" is the tenth episode of the television program Lois & Clark: The New Adventures of Superman.

[edit] Summary

A heatwave in the middle of winter in Metropolis is being blamed on Superman. Lois has to figure out who is really causing it, while Clark has a crisis of faith.

[edit] Main cast

  • Lane Smith as Perry White
  • Tracy Scoggins as Cat Grant

  • K Callan as Martha Kent

[edit] Guest starring

  • Rosalind Cash as Judge Angela Diggs
  • Sonny Bono as Mayor Berkowitz
  • Richard Fancy as Dr. Edward Saxon
  • Elaine Kagan as Dr. Katherine Goodman
  • Haunani Minn as the city attorney

[edit] Production details

  • The mayor (played by Sonny Bono) says "The heat goes on" and "I got you, babe" to Lois, referring to two of Bono's songs, "The Beat Goes On" and "I Got You, Babe."
