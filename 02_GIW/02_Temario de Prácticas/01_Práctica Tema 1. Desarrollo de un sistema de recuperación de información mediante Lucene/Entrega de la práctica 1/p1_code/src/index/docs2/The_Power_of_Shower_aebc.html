The Power of Shower is the first full-length studio album from Dan Bﾄネan's Crazy Loop act. The album was released on December 2, 2007. [1] He is best known for being part of the O-Zone pop band.

The album is produced by himself and features his hit single "Crazy Loop". The album features a mix of both dance and rock songs in both English and Moldovan.

His hit single "Crazy Loop" had a CD single release in March of 2008.

The album is set for international releases later in the year. [2]
