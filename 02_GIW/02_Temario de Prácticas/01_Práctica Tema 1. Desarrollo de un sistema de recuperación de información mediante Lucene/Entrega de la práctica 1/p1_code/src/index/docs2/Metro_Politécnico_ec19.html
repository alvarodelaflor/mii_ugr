Metro Politécnico is a station on the Mexico City Metro. It is located in the north of Mexico City, in Gustavo A. Madero borough. It is the northern terminus for Line 5.

The station's logo is the emblem of Instituto Politécnico Nacional, one of the most important universities in México. It is represented by a half cogwheel, the stylised form of a building and the acronym IPN.

This station is located under 100 Metros avenue, also, it is located near Nueva Industrial Vallejo neighbourhood, and some workshops of bus lines like Omnibus de México, ETN and Estrella Blanca.
