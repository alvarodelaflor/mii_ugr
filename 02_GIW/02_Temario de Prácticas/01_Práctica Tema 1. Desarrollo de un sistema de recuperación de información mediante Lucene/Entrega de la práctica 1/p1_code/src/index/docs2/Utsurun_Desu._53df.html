Utsurun Desu.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Kawauso-kun running in the manga Uturun Desu.

Utsurun Desu. (伝染るんです。) is a Japanese comic strip or manga created by Sensha Yoshida, which ran on Big Comic Spirits issued by Shogakukan from 1989 to 1994. This manga is deemed to be one of the works that initiated the "irrational" or "unreasonable" gag comic genre where the reason of its funniness cannot be explained by logic and traditional sense of humor, which made this title very popular among teens and early twenties at that time. One of the most popular characters that appeared in Uturun Desu was "the otter", Kawauso-kun.

Utsurun Desu. roughly means "it infects you" and also is the synonym for a disposable camera produced by Fujifilm (where it means "it can take photos").

In 1991 a video game was released by Shogakukan Productions Co.,Ltd. about Kawauso-kun journeying to Hawaii, titled Utsurun Desu - Kawauso Hawaii e iku or Utsurun Desu - Kawauso move's to Hawaii.
