Time Radio was a local pirate station based in North West London, UK launched on the 4 August 1982 in Harlesden above Eric & son from Ella Fitzgerald concert at the Royal Festival Hall in London. The first show was broadcast by Ges Nelson followed by Mickey D. The station ended with an application for a legitimate broadcasting local licence.

Disc jockeys on the station included DJ Blacka, Mickey D, Roland Rat, Supes, Special K, Noel Callendar, Daddy Ernie, Ges Nelson, Lyndon T, The Dude, Keith James, Chris Johns, JC, Jookie Jam, Cool and Deadly, Vego Wells, Chris Nat, Gamos Speng, Errol T, Natty B, Joe Guy, Peaches, Tiger, Doctor D, Sparkey, Gemma, Boogie Boy, Kidd Nice, Microchip, D.J., Tony T and Quincey

Through dispute the station split into two stations and the new one named Fresh FM
