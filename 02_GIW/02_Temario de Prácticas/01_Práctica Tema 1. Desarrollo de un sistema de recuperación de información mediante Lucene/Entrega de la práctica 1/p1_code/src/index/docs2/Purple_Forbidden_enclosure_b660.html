Purple Forbidden enclosure

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Zǐ Wēi Yuán map

Zi Wei Yuan, the Purple Forbidden Enclosure (紫微垣) is one of the San Yuan or Three enclosures. Stars and constellations of this group lie near the north celestial pole and visible all year from temperate latitudes in the Northern Hemisphere.
