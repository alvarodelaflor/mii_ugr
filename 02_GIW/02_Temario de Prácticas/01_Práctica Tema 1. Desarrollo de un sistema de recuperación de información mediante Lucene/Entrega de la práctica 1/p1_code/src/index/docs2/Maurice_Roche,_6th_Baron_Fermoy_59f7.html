Maurice Roche, 6th Baron Fermoy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Patrick Maurice Burke Roche, 6th Baron Fermoy (11 October 1967) is a British peer. He is the only child of Edmund Burke Roche, 5th Baron Fermoy and his wife, the former Lavinia Pitman. Lord Fermoy is also a cousin of the late Diana, Princess of Wales. He gained the title of 6th Baron Fermoy when his father died in 1984.

On 26 March 1998, he married Tessa Fiona Kayll, daughter of Major David Pelham Kayll and his wife, Sheila Morrison. Baron Fermoy and Tessa Kayll have two daughters:

  • The Hon. Arabella Elizabeth Burke Roche, born in 18 March 1999
  • The Hon. Eliza Lavinia Burke Roche, born in 9 November 2000

The wife of Baron Fermoy also has two children from her former marriage with Simon Ledger.
