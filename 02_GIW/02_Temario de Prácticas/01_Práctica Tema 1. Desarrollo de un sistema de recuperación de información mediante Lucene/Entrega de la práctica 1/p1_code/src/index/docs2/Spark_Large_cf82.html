Spark Large is the first release by Swedish, indie rock band Marching Band. The scheduled release date is August 5, 2008 by U & L Records.

[edit] Production

Spark Large was recorded from January, 2008 through April, 2008 in Studio City, CA at Fireproof Recording. It was mixed and engineered by Adam Lasus, produced by Adam Lasus and Marching Band and Mastered by Greg Calbi at Sterling Sound.
