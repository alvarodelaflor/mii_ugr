Arnaud Casquette

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arnaud Casquette (born 16 April 1978) is a Mauritian long jumper.

He finished seventh at the 2002 Commonwealth Games.[1] He also competed at the 1996 and 2000 Summer Olympics.

His personal best jump is 8.13 metres, achieved in July 2003 in Sestriere. The Mauritian record is currently held by Jonathan Chimier with 8.28 metres.[2]

At the 2005 Jeux de la Francophonie he tested positive for the banned substance cannabis and was suspended between December 2005 and June 2006.[3]
