The only single released from Diecast's second full-length effort, Day of Reckoning, released in 2001 on Now or Never Records. Also in 2001 the song was chosen as the title track for the video game Ultimate Fighting Championship: Tapout. The game was released for Playstation 2 and Xbox.

[edit] Video

Directed by Marc Mirnbach the video featured then new singer Paul Stoddard on vocals. It was a live video shot at Stoddard's first two shows with the band in 2003, one in Maine and the other in Worcester, MA. The two shows were then to be edited together and a new recorded version of the song with Paul's voice was supposed to be dubbed over the edited video. This never happened, so the band posted the edited video "as is" on their website for fans to download.
