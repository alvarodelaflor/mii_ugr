Agrate Conturbia is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Novara in the Italian region Piedmont, located about 100 km northeast of Turin and about 25 km north of Novara. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,351 and an area of 14.5 km².[1]

The municipality of Agrate Conturbia contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Agrate and Conturbia.

Agrate Conturbia borders the following municipalities: Bogogno, Borgo Ticino, Divignano, Mezzomerico, Suno, and Veruno.
