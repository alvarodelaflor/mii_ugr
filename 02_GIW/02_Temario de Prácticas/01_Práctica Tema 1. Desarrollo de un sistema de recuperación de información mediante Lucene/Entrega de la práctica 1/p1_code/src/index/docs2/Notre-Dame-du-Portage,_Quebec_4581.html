Notre-Dame-du-Portage

Notre-Dame-du-Portage is a village in the Canadian Province of Quebec located at the edge of the Saint Lawrence River in the Bas-Saint-Laurent region. It is part of the Rivière-du-Loup Regional County Municipality and home to the Riviere-du-Loup Golf Club.

The Roman Catholic Church seen here was constructed in 1859.

Notre-Dame-du-Portage is a member of the Association of the Most Beautiful Villages of Quebec.
