Satomi Sato

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Satomi Sato (佐藤聡美, Satō Satomi, born May 8, 1986) is a voice actress from Miyagi Prefecture, Japan affiliated with Aoni Production's Junior Talent. She is a graduate of Tokyo Announce Gakuin Performing Arts College. She is also known as Sugar (しゅがぁ, Shugā).

[edit] Achievements

While attending Tokyo Announce Gakuin, Sato was selected to be in the seventh line-up of assistants (Big Bang Grand Prix V) on Miki Tomokazu's Radio Big Bang. As an assistant, she formed the unit Kisty.
