Argon (1908 automobile)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Argon was made by Grannaway Engineering Co, Earls Court, London S.W. in 1908. It was a fairly large touring car using a 25 hp 6-cylinder Coventry-Simplex engine. It was priced at 750 pounds. In 1905, the company was said to be planning ac car deemed the Grannaway, but it is not clear whether this ever came to fruition.
