Mike DeVito (born June 10, 1984) is an American football Nose tackle and Defensive end for the New York Jets. He signed as an undrafted free agent in 2007 and won a roster spot over established veterans Bobby Hamilton and Kimo Von Oelhoffen. He played college football at The University of Maine. He finished his career at Maine with 112 total tackles along with 16 sacks and served as team captain his senior season.
