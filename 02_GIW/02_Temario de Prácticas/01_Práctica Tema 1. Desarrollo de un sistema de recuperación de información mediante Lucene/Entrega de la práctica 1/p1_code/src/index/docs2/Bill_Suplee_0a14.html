Bill Suplee

Occupation Stage, film actor

William "Bill" Suplee (born 1940) is an American actor. He is the father of actor Ethan Suplee with whom he co-starred in My Name is Earl.

[edit] Acting career

Bill started his career while working with the Summer stock theatre and on Broadway, where he meet his wife.

He got his first major role in My Name is Earl as Willie "The One Eyed Mailman".

He is also set to appear in the film Knife to a Gun Fight in 2008.
