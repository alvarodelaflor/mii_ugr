Panorama (German wartime newsreel)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Panorama was a German quarterly color newsreel produced from 1944 until the end of World War 2.[citation needed]

[edit] Panorama subjects

Panorama supposedly focused on "human interest" stories in 1944, such as harvesting but also army training.[citation needed]

Several short clips can be found on Youtube.
