Revelles is a commune in the Somme département in the Picardie region of France.

[edit] Geography

Situated around 8 miles southwest of Amiens, on the D51 and D97 crossroads.

[edit] History

A Gallo-Roman settlement was unearthed within the confines of the commune, during the construction of the A29 motorway. [1]
