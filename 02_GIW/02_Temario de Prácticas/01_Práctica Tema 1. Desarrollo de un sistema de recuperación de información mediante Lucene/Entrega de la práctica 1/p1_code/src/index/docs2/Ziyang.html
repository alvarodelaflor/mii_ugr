Ziyang

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Ziyang City jurisdiction (yellow) within Sichuan

Ziyang (simplified Chinese: 资阳; traditional Chinese: 資陽; pinyin: Zīyáng; Wade-Giles: Tzu-yang) prefecture-level city in the Sichuan Province, in south central China, with more than 100,000 inhabitants.
