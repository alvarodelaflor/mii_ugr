Wheel of Fortune

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Wheel of Fortune may refer to the popular television game show franchise:

  • Wheel of Fortune (US game show), the current, syndicated US version.
  • Wheel of Fortune (US daytime game show), the former, daytime US version.
  • Wheel of Fortune 2000, a former American kids' version.
  • Wheel of Fortune (UK game show), the United Kingdom version.
  • Wheel of Fortune (Australian game show), the original Australian version.
  • Million Dollar Wheel of Fortune, the revived Australian version.
  • Wheel of Fortune (Philippine game show), the Philippine version.
  • La Roue de la Fortune, the French version.
  • Pole Chudes, the Russian version.
  • Wheel of Fortune in different countries, information on all international versions of the game show franchise
  • Wheel of Fortune (The wheel's configuration), the current configuration of the wheel on the American version
  • Wheel of Fortune (video game), the video game based on the game show franchise

Wheel of Fortune may also refer to:

    • Hence, the Wheel of Fortune (Tarot card)
  • Wheel of Fortune (song), recorded by Kay Starr
  • Wheel of Fortune (Ace of Base song)
  • Wheel of Fortune (Dead Zone), the pilot episode of The Dead Zone

  • Wheel of Fortune House, a historic building in Kent County, Delaware, United States
