Celso Otero

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Celso Otero Quintás (born February 1, 1959 in Montevideo) is a retired football goalkeeper from Uruguay, who obtained one official cap for the Uruguay national football team: on August 7, 1988 in a friendly against Colombia (1-2) in Bogotá. He was a stand-in for his native country at the 1986 FIFA World Cup, wearing the number 22 jersey.

[edit] Clubs

  • Club Nacional de Football (1978-1979)
  • Racing Club de Montevideo (1980)
  • Alto Perú (1981)
  • Montevideo Wanderers F.C. (1982-1988)
