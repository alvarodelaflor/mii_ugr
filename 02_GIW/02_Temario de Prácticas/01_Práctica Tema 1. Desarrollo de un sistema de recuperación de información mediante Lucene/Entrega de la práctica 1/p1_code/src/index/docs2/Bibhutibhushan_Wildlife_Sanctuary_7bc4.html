Bibhutibhushan Wildlife Sanctuary

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bibhutibhusan Wildlife Sanctuary is situated in North 24 Parganas district, West Bengal has a healthy population of spotted deer and the sanctuary is also rich in common birds.
