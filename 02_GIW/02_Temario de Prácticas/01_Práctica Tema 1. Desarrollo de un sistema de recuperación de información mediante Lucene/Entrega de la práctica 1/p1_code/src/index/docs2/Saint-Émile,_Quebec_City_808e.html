Saint-Émile, Quebec City

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Saint-Émile is a former city in central Quebec, Canada. It was amalgamated into Quebec City on January 1, 2002. It is located within the Borough of La Haute-Saint-Charles. Population: (2001) 9,889.
