Mário Gil Fernandes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mário Gil Fernandes (born April 25, 1982, Funchal, Madeira) is a Portuguese basketball player, currently playing for Spanish side CB Plasencia, having previously played in local club CAB Madeira and Murcia's Balneario de Archena. He measures 1.74 metres and plays as a point guard.
