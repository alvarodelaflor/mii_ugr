Tenon Group plc is a chartered accountancy company in the United Kingdom. The company has 39 offices across the UK, employing over 1,800 staff. Key services offered include auditing, consultancy,business recovery and tax services to a wide variety of organisations in the private and public sectors. The group have recently branched out and now offer Business Continuity and yachting and aviation services.

Tenon Group is the 9th largest accountancy firm in the UK, with revenues of £137.1 million for the year ending 30 June 2007, and average revenue per director of £700,000. [1]
