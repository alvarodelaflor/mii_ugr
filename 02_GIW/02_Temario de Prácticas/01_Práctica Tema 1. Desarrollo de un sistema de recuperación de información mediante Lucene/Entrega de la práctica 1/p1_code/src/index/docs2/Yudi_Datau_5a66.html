Yudi Datau

Born  Indonesia
Years active 2003 -

Yudi Datau is an Indonesian cinematographer who has worked on several of Indonesia's most popular films.

In 2003 he worked on Arisan! and in 2006 Denias Senandung Di Atas Awan.

[edit] Filmography

  • Arisan! (2003)
  • Impian kemarau (2004)
  • Ungu Violet (2005)
  • Gie (2005)
  • Denias Senandung Di Atas Awan (2006)
  • Nagabonar jadi 2 (2007)
