Caluquembe

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Caluquembe (Kalukembe) is an Angolan town in Angola's Huíla Province. It is also the site of two missionary efforts; one, Roman Catholic and the other, Evangelical Protestant. At the Protestant mission there were for the majority of the past century a Bible school and a hospital run by the Swiss Alliance Missionaire Evangelique.
