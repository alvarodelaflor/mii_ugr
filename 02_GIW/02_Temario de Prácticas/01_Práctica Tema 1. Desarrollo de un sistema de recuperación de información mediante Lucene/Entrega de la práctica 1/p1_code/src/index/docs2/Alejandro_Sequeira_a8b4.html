Alejandro Sequeira (born 27 April 1975) is a Costa Rican football player currently playing for Brujas. He formerly played in Costa Rica for Deportivo Saprissa, Santa Barbara, Cartaginés and Carmelita, in Guatemala for Cobán Imperial, in the United States for Tampa Bay and San Jose, in Greece for Proodeftiki, and in El Salvador for Águila.
