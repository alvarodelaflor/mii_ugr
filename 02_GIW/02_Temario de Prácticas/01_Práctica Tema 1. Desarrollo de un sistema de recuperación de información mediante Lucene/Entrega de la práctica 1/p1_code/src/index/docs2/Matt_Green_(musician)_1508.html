Matt Green (musician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Matt Green (born March 27, 1967 in Queens, New York) is a keyboard player, song writer and producer, best known for his association with the Los Angeles-based electronic-industrial music group Spahn Ranch from 1992 to 2000. Aside from his work with Spahn Ranch, Green produced several other albums as well as remixing over 70 records for other artists during his stint living in Los Angeles.
