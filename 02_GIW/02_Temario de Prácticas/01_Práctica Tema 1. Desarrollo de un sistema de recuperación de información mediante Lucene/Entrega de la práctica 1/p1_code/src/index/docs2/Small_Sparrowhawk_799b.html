The Small Sparrowhawk, Épervier Des Célèbes, or Gavilancito De Célebes (Accipiter nanus) is a species of bird of prey in the Accipitridae family. It is endemic to Indonesia. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and subtropical or tropical moist montanes. It is threatened by habitat loss.
