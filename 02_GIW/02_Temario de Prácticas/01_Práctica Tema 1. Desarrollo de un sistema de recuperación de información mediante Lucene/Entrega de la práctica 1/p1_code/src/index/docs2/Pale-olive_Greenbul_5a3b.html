The Pale-olive Greenbul (Phyllastrephus fulviventris) is a species of songbird in the Pycnonotidae family. It is found in Angola, Democratic Republic of the Congo, and Gabon. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry forests, subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, and moist savanna.
