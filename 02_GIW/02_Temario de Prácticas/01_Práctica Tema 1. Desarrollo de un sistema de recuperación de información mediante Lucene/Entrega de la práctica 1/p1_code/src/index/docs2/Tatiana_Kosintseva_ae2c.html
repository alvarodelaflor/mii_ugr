Tatiana Kosintseva (born April 11, 1986) is a Russian chess player who has achieved the FIDE Woman Grandmaster and International Master title. As of April 2008 2008 her FIDE ELO rating is 2497. Her peak rating is 2502 so far (July 2007).

In 2004 and 2007 she won the Russian women's chess championship. In 2006 she and her sister Nadezhda came equal second in the Russian women's superfinal half a point behind the winner.

She was the winner of the 2007 European Individual Championship (women) with a performance rating of 2774.

Her older sister, IM Nadezhda Kosintseva, is also a chess player.
