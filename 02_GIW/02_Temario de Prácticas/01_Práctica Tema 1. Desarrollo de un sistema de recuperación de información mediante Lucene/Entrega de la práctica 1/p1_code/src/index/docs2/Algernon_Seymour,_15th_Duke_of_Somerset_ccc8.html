Algernon Seymour, 15th Duke of Somerset

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Algernon St. Maur, 15th Duke of Somerset (1846 – October 29, 1923) was the son of Algernon St. Maur, 14th Duke of Somerset and Horatia Morler.

On September 5, 1877, he married Susan Margaret Richards Mackinnon. Because St. Maur did not have any children, his titles passed to his nearest relative, Edward Seymour.
