Marcel Granollers Pujol (born April 12, 1986, in Barcelona) is a professional tennis player from Spain who turned professional in 2003. As of June 20, 2007, Granollers Pujol was ranked number 184 in the singles rankings. He reached his highest singles ranking of World Number 51 on May 26, 2008, and his highest doubles ranking of World Number 51.
