Su-ngai Padi (Thai: สุไหงปาดี) is a district (Amphoe) in Narathiwat Province, Thailand.

[edit] Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the north clockwise) Cho-airong, Tak Bai, Su-ngai Kolok, Waeng, Sukhirin and Ra-ngae.

[edit] Administration

The district is subdivided into 6 subdistricts (tambon), which are further subdivided into 50 villages (muban). Paluru is a township (thesaban tambon) which covers parts of the tambon Paluru. There are further 6 Tambon administrative organizations (TAO).
