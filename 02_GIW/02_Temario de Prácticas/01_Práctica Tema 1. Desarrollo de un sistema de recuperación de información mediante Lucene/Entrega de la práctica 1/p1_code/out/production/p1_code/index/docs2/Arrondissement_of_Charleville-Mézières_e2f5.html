The arrondissement of Charleville-Mézières is an arrondissement of France, located in the Ardennes département, in the Champagne-Ardenne région. It has 17 cantons and 160 communes.

[edit] Cantons

The cantons of the arrondissement of Charleville-Mézières are:

  8. Monthermé

  10. Omont

  13. Rocroi
  14. Rumigny
  15. Signy-l'Abbaye
  16. Signy-le-Petit
  17. Villers-Semeuse
