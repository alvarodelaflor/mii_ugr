Robert C. Zimmerman

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Robert C. Zimmerman (January 5, 1910 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin - January 17, 1996 in Milwaukee) was Secretary of State of Wisconsin from 1957 until his retirement in 1975.[1]

His father Fred R. Zimmerman was Governor of Wisconsin and later Secretary of State of Wisconsin.
