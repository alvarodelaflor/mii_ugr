Anne Howard was born on March 18 1925, in Chicago, Illinois, USA. She was married to Leonard Caulfield (?-1980) and they had one daughter, Vicki Caulfied. Anne retired from acting in 1966, then became active in civic affairs. She died on April 22, 1991, in Los Angeles, California, of a cerebral hemorrhage.
