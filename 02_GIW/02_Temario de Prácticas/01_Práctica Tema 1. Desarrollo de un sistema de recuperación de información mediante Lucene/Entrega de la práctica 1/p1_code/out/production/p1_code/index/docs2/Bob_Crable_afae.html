Bob Crable (born September 22, 1959, in Cincinnati, Ohio) is a former linebacker who played for the New York Jets. He was an All American linebacker at Moeller High School while playing under the legendary coach Gerry Faust and an All-American at Notre Dame. He still holds the record for most career tackles for the Irish. Currently, he is a religion teacher at his alma mater,[1]. Bob was a standout at Moeller High School when he played linebacker there. He also coached "Big Moe" for seven years from 2000 to 2007. Unfortunately he was fired
