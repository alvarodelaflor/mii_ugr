Anth was a 1994 action movie starring Sunil Shetty as Vijay, Somy Ali as Priya, Paresh Rawal as Dabala, and Deepak Shirke as Inspector Shirke. Mohan Joshi, Markand Deshpande, Alok Nath and Vijendra Ghaat are also featured in the film. The movie was directed by Sanjay Khanna, and produced by Ashok Honda. The film's music was provided by Anand-Milind.

[edit] Plot

Kali (played by Markand Deshpande) is a local ruffian who harasses students in his college. The principal Satyaprakash (played by Alok Nath) is helpless to stop him because Kali is the son of Dabala (played by Paresh Rawal) who is one of the biggest gangsters in town. The principal has 2 sons and 1 daughter - Vikas (Vijendra Ghaat), Vijay (Sunil Shetty) and Pooja. Vijay is in love with Priya (Somi Ali) a colleague of his in his engineering company.

Kali rapes Pooja, and then the corrupt inspector Shirke (Deepak Shirke) puts the blame on Pooja's fiancee, and has him killed while in custody. This enrages Vijay and he embarks upon a bloody and violent fight to cleanse the city of ruffians like Kali and Dabala and Shirke. Mohan Joshi shows up in a cameo as an honest ACP.

[edit] Reception

The movie was a success at the box-office; that was mostly due to the action sequences featuring Sunil Shetty[citation needed]. One particular long action sequence features Shetty and Ali on a motorcycle, with Shetty clenching a stun-gun in his hand.
