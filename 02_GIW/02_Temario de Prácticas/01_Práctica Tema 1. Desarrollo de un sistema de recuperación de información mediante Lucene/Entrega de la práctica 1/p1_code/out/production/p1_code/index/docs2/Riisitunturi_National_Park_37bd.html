Riisitunturi National Park (Riisitunturin kansallispuisto) is a national park in Lapland Province, Finland. It was established in 1982 and covers 77 square kilometres (30 mi²). The park is on a mountainous area, and there are also many swamps, especially hillside swamps.

The only wilderness hut in the park is located near the twin-peak Riisitunturi (465.3 m).
