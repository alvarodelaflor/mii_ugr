Accuride Corporation (Illinois), an unrelated company, is a manufacturer of vehicle wheels.

Accuride International Inc. is a manufacturer of drawer slides for residential and industrial applications,[1] based in Santa Fe Springs, California, USA and has about 500 employees.[2] It had a manufacturing plant in Charlotte, North Carolina, USA, which closed in 2001 with the loss of 150-200 jobs.[3]

Accuride is the best-known maker of drawer slides among woodworkers, and is particularly known for its full-extension slides.[4] Accuride holds dozens of patents in drawer slide technology.[5]
