Saarijärvi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Saarijärvi (disambiguation).

Saarijärvi is a municipality of Finland. It is located in the province of Western Finland and is part of the Central Finland region. The municipality has a population of 10,205 (2003) and covers an area of 1,030.37 km² of which 142.97 km² is water. The population density is 11.5 inhabitants per km².

The municipality is unilingually Finnish.

  • It is the home of Bonden Paavo in the poem by Johan Ludvig Runeberg.
  • Juho Hyytiäinen, the great grandfather of Pamela Anderson's left the village in 1908 emigrating to the American continent.
