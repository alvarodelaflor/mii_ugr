Bures (pronounced /ˈbʊɚz/) is on the Essex/Suffolk border and is formed of two civil parishes. These are

  • Bures Hamlet (Essex)
  • Bures St. Mary (Suffolk)

Alan Beales a local resident has spent the last ten years documenting the village history from 1900 up to the present day. In particular looking at the lost retail outlets and local industries that have disappeared during this time. Read all about how the River with its "lighters" (barges) and how the Railway played an important part in transporting goods to and from the village

The site contains over 200 pages of local information

Look at the Bures Home page(http://www.bures-online.co.uk) for more information.

There is also a collection of some 200+ images of village life at his other web site at http://www.bures.org.uk
