[edit] Events

[edit] By Place

[edit] Eastern Roman Empire

  • Battle of the Utus: Attila the Hun meets the Romans in an indecisive battle. The Huns invade the Balkans as far as Thermopylae.
  • Sofia is destroyed by the Huns.
  • Earthquakes level most of the walls of Constantinople, which are rebuilt within sixty days.

[edit] Europe

  • Merovech becomes king of the Franks.
  • The first English kingdom in Britain is created when Vortigern grants Thanet in Kent to the Saxon leader Hengist.

[edit] By Topic

[edit] Arts and Sciences

  • The first entry in the Annales Cambriæ refers to this year.

[edit] Religion

  • Flavian becomes bishop of Constantinople.
  • Synod of Toledo: The filioque clause is added to the Nicene Creed.
