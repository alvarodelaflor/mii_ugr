Mike Fleiss

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Mike Fleiss is the creator, producer, and writer of The WB's Superstar USA, The Bachelorette, and High School Reunion. He was also the producer of the 2003 remake of The Texas Chainsaw Massacre (2003 film) and The Texas Chainsaw Massacre: The Beginning.
