Maus (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Maus may refer to:

  • "Maus", the German word for "mouse"
  • Maus, a series of Pulitzer Prize-winning graphic novels by Art Spiegelman
  • Maus (band), an Icelandic rock band
  • Panzer VIII Maus, a name for the German World War II Panzerkampfwagen VIII tank prototype, the heaviest tank ever built
  • Die Sendung mit der Maus, a children's television show from Germany
  • Burg Maus, a castle in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany
