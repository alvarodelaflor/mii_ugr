Mike Burgener is a Senior International Weightlifting Coach for the United States. 1


[edit] Key Facts

-Played American football for the University of Notre Dame 1

-Served in the United States Marine Corps 1

-Is the Strength and Conditioning Coach for Rancho Buena Vista High School

-Runs a Regional Training Center for USA Weightlifting from Bonsall, California 2

-Coaches the Junior World Women's Weightlifting Team 1

-His son, Casey Burgener, is a notable American Olympic Weightlifter
