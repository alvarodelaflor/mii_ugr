Bourdon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bourdon can mean:

  • A bourdon is a type of bell
  • A bourdon is a type of organ pipe, though may also refer to other meanings of the term diapason
  • Bourdon, Somme, a town in France
  • A Bourdon gauge or Bourdon tube is used in pressure measurement, invented by Eugene Bourdon. See Pressure measurement#Bourdon


Bourdon is also a surname and may refer to:

  • Amé Bourdon, 17th century French physician
  • Eugene Bourdon, French watchmaker and engineer
  • François Louis Bourdon, 18th century French politician
  • Luc Bourdon an NHL defenseman with the Vancouver Canucks.
  • Rob Bourdon, the drummer of Linkin Park
  • William Bourdon the secretary-general of the International Federation of Human Rights Leagues
