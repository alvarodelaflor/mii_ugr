Sara Powell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sara Powell is a Jamaican-British actress who appeared in the BBC drama The Family Man. She has also appeared in Judge John Deed, Doctors, Silent Witness and the BBC's 1998 version of Vanity Fair. She is currently a regular in the BBC police drama Holby Blue. Powell is married with one daughter.

Her theatre credits include World Music (Donmar Warehouse), Stuff Happens (National Theatre), Macbeth (The Almeida Theatre), and Some Girl(s) (Gielgud Theatre).
