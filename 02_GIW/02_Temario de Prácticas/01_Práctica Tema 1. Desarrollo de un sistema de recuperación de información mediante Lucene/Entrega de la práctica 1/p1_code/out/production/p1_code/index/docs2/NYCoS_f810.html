NYCoS

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

NYCoS (The National Youth Choir of Scotland). Founded by Christopher Bell and established in 1996 to provide a forum for young people to sing to the highest possible standards, the organisation has dramatically expanded its activities since then to include the main NYCoS choir for singers aged 16-24, NYCoS Training Choir for less experienced singers aged 14-18, a National Boys Choir and a National Girls Choir, and a network of eight Area Choirs (which include Dumfries, Dundee, Edinburgh, Falkirk, Galloway, Renfrewshire, Stirling and West Lothian) books, CDs, in-service days and Singing Days. 2006 marked the choir's 10th anniversary and has seen them performing in the BBC proms at the Royal Albert Hall along with many other venues.
