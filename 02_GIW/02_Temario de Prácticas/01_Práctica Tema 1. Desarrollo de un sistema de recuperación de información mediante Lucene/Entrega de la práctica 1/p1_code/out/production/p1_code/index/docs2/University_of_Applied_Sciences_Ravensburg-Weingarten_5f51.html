Ravensburg-Weingarten University of Applied Science (German: Hochschule Ravensburg-Weingarten) is a Public University in the city of Weingarten, in south of German state of Baden-Württemberg. The university was founded in 1962 and tracks advanced fields in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Automotive and Mechatronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Business & Technology Management. With not a big campus and not a big population (about 4,500 students), however, it is offering a very challenging academic environment in south of High Technology Land, Baden-Württemberg.
