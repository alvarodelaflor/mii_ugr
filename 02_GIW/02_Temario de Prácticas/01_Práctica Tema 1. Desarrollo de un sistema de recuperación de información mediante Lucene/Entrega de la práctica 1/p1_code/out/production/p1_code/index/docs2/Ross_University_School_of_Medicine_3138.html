Ross University School of Medicine (RUSOM) is a medical school located in Dominica. It grants the Doctor of Medicine degree after 4 years of study.

[edit] Accreditation

RUSOM is recognized and accredited by the Dominica Medical Board.[citation needed]

RUSOM is listed in the Foundation for Advancement of International Medical Education and Research (FAIMER) International Medical Education Directory (IMED) [1]

[edit] Licensing

Based on a 2004 site visit from the California Medical Board, graduates from Ross University are allowed to apply for a medical license in California. [2]

The State of New York has approved Ross University Graduates to apply for a medical license. [3]

There are Ross University Graduates practicing medicine in all 50 States[citation needed].
