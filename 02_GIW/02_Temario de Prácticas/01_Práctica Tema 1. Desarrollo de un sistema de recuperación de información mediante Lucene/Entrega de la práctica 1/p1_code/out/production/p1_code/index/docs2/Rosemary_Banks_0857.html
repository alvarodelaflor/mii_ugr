Rosemary Banks

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Main article: New Zealand and the United Nations

Rosemary Banks has been New Zealand's Permanent Representative to the United Nations in New York since 8 June 2005.

She graduated with an MA from the University of Canterbury, and received an MSc from the London School of Economics in the UK. She is married to Brian Lockstone.
