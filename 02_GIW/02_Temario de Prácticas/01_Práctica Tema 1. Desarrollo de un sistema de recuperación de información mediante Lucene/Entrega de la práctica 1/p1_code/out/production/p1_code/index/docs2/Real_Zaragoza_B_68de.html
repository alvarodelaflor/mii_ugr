Real Zaragoza B is the reserve team of Real Zaragoza. Founded in 1965, it plays in the Tercera División - Group 17. Its stadium is Ciudad Deportiva with a capacity of 2,500 seats.

[edit] Club names

  • Deportivo Aragón - (1965–1991)
  • Real Zaragoza B - (1991–)

[edit] Seasons

  • 2004/2005: Segunda División B 5th
  • 2005/2006: Segunda División B 19th - Relegated
  • 2006/2007: Tercera División 1st
  • 2007/2008: Tercera División 6th
  • 2008/2009: Tercera División
  • 1 seasons in Segunda División
  • 20 seasons in Segunda División B
  • 17 seasons in Tercera División
