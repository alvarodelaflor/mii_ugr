Richard Piers Rayner is an English comic book artist. He is best known for his work on Road to Perdition.

[edit] Biography

Richard Piers Rayner began his professional comic career in 1988, illustrating for DC Comics and Marvel Comics. His works include, Hellblazer, Swamp Thing, L.E.G.I.O.N., Doctor Fate, and Doctor Who. His most notable work is Road to Perdition, which was adapted into a movie in 2002, starring Tom Hanks, and directed by Sam Mendes.[1]

He is also a frequent contributor to the DC publications, Paradox Press.
