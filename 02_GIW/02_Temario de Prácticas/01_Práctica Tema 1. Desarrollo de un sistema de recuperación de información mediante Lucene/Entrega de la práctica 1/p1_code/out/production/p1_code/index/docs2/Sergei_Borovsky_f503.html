Sergei Borovsky

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sergei Borovsky (born 29 January 1956) is a former football player and current manager.

During his club career he played for FC Dinamo Minsk. He earned 21 caps and scored 1 goal for the USSR national football team, and participated in the 1982 FIFA World Cup.

He managed the Belarus national football team from 1994 to 1996 and from 1999 to 2000.
