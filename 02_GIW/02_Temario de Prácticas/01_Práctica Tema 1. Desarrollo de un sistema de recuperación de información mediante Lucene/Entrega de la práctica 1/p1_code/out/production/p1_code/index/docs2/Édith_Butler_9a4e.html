Édith Butler

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Édith Butler O.C. (born Marie Nicole Butler 27 July 1942 in Paquetville, New Brunswick) is an Acadian singer-songwriter and folklorist. Her career began in the early 1960s with performances in Moncton, followed by national appearances on CBC Television's Singalong Jubilee.

2003's Madame Butlerfly is her most recent studio recording.

Butler was appointed an Officer of the Order of Canada in June 1975.
