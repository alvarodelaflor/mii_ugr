Anna Morton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anna Livingston Reade Street Morton (May 18, 1846—August 14, 1918) was the second wife of Vice President Levi P. Morton. She was known as Anna Street Morton.

She married Vice President Morton in 1873 and they had five daughters.

Anna Street Morton was Second Lady of the United States (1889-1893) and First Lady of New York (1895-1897).
