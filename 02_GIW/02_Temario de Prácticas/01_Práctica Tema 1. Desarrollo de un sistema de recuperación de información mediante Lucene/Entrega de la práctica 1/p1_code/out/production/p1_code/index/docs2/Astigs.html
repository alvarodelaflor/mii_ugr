'Astigs' is ABS-CBN's weekly youth-oriented program running under the youth anthology series "Star Magic Presents" which started airing on January 12[1], 2008. The show will feature different stories every six weeks.

[edit] Season 1: Astigs in Haay...School Life

The initial offering of Astigs, Astigs in Haay...School Life, revolves around five outsiders trying to fit in with theyone who dares to be different[2]..

[edit] Season 2: Astigs in Luvin' Lyf

Epi just broke with Janine. To help Epi recover from his heartbreak his friends introduces him to Reema, a hot chick who rides a bike[3]. Reema, dumped by his boyfriend for another girp grows. Reema tells Epi to talk things out with Janine and Epi confronts Jethro about Reema.
