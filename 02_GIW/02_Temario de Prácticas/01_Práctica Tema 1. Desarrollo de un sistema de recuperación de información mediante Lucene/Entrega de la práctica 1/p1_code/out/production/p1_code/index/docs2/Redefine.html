Redefine is the third album by the Chicago-based music group SOiL. The album was released on March 23, 2004 via J Records. This was to be the band's final album with vocalist Ryan McCombs. The album was SOiL's last with J Records, after the band was dropped due to disappointing sales. The album peaked at #78 on the Billboard 200 album charts. "Pride" was featured in the video game Madden NFL 2004. According to the cover booklet contained within the album, the track Remember was dedicated to the memory of Dave Williams. All tracks recorded at Groovemaster Studios, Chicago, Illinois, USA unless otherwise noted.
