Ōmi, Shiga

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ōmi (近江町, Ōmi-chō?) was a town located in Sakata District, Shiga, Japan. On October 1, 2005, Ōmi merged into the city of Maibara.

As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 9,663 and a density of 535.64 persons per km². The total area was 18.04 km².
