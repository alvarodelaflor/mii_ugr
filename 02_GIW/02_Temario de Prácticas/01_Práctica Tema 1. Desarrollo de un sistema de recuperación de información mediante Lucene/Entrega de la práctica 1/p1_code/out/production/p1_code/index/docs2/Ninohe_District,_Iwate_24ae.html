Ninohe District, Iwate

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ninohe (二戸郡, Ninohe-gun?) is a district located in Iwate, Japan.

As of December 1, 2005, the district has an estimated population of 15,821. The total area is 300.11km².

The district has only one town left.

  • Ichinohe

[edit] District Timeline

  • April 1, 2002-The town of Ashiro was transferred to Iwate District.
  • January 1, 2006-The town of Jōbōji merged with the city of Ninohe to create the new city of Ninohe.
