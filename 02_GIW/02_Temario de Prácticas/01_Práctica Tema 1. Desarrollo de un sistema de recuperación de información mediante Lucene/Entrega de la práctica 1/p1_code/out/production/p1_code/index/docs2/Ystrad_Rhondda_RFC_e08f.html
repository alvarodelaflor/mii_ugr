Ystrad Rhondda Rugby Football Club is a Welsh rugby union team based in Ystrad in the Rhondda Valley. Ystrad Rhondda RFC play home games in royal and blue hooped shirts with black shorts and black socks. Today, Ystrad Rhondda RFC plays in the Welsh Rugby Union Division Two East league and are a feeder club for Cardiff Blues.[2]

[edit] Club honours

  • 2004-05 WRU Division Two East Champions
  • 2007-08 WRU Division Two East Champions
