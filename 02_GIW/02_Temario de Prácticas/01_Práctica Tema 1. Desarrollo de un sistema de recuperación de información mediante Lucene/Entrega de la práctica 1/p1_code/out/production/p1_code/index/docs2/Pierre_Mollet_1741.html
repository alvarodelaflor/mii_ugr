Pierre Mollet

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Pierre Mollet (born 23 March 1920) is a Swiss baritone. He made his debut in 1952, singing the male lead in Pelléas et Mélisande at the Opéra-Comique. He created the role of Eraste in Frank Martin's opera Monsieur de Pourceaugnac; other significant roles include Albert in Massenet's Werther and Mercutio in Gounod's Roméo et Juliette.

In 1974 he became a naturalized citizen of Canada, and in 1979 married the Canadian pianist Suzanne Blondin.
