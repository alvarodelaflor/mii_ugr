Adam Michael Kraus (born September 10, 1984) is an American football guard for the Baltimore Ravens of the NFL.

[edit] Collegiate Career

At Michigan, Kraus was a four-year letterman who appeared in 39 career games, making 35 starts along the offensive line. Adam started 26 straight games to finish his career, 21 at left guard and the final five contests at center. He is a two-time All-Big Ten first team selection in 2006 and 2007.[1]

[edit] Professional Career

Adam was signed by the Ravens as a rookie free agent on May 9, 2008.[2]
