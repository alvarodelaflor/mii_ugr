Church in Častkov

Častkov is a village and municipality in Senica District in the Trnava Region of western Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1394.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 280 metres and covers an area of 13.182km². It has a population of about 610 people.
