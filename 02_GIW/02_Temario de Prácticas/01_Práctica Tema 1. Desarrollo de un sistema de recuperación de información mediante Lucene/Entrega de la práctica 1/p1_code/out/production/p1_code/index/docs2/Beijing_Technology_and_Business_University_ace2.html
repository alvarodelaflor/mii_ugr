Beijing Technology and Business University

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Beijing Technology and Business University (北京工商大学) is a university in Beijing, China. It was established in June 1999 by the merging of the Beijing Institute of Light Industry, the Beijing Institute of Business, and the Institute of Managerial Cadres of Machinery Industry.
