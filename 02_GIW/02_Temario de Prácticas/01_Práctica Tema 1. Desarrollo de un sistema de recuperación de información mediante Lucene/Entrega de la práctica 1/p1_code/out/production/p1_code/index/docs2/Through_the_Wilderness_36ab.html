Through the Wilderness

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Through the Wilderness
Tribute album by Various artists
Genre Pop
Folk Rock
Label Manimal Vinyl Records

Through the Wilderness is a tribute album to Madonna by various folk artists, released on November 27, 2007 on Manimal Vinyl Records.

  • Professional Reviews
  • 7 out of 10- Pitchforkmedia.com
  • 3.5 Stars (out of 4)- Spin Magazine


[edit] Tracks included

  • "La Isla Bonita", by Jonathan Wilson
  • "Into the Groove", by Jeremy Jay
  • "Beautiful Stranger", by Golden Animals
  • "Live to Tell", by Winter Flowers
  • "Material Girl", by Mountain Party
  • "Everybody", by Ariel Pink
  • "Oh Father", by Giant Drag
  • "Hung Up", by The Tyde
  • "Lucky Star", by Alexandra Hope
  • "Borderline", by The Chapin Sisters
  • "Dress You Up", by Apollo Heights
  • "Who's That Girl", by The Bubonic Plague
  • "Cherish", by The Prayers
  • "Crazy for You", by Lion of Panjshir
  • "Like a Prayer", by Lavender Diamond
  • "Holiday", by Siddhartha (to be released on iTunes only)
  • "Impressive Instant", by Pangaeans (iTunes only)
  • Manimal Vinyl Records. Los Angeles based record label founded in 2006.
