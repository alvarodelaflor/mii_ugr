Maryland Route 658 is a north/south route located in the U.S. state of Maryland. The route runs from Maryland Route 53 in LaVale, Allegany County north to U.S. Route 40 Alternate in LaVale, Allegany County. North of the Interstate 68 interchange the road is named Campground Road, and south of the interchange it is named Vocke Road.

The section of Route 658 south of Interstate 68 serves as part of U.S. Route 220 Truck, an alternate route for trucks which avoids a steep ramp onto southbound U.S. Route 220 at exit 42 on Interstate 68.

[edit] Route description

Maryland Route 658 begins as Vocke Road[1] at Maryland Route 53[1] (Winchester Road)[1] in LaVale, Maryland.[1] It is a four-to-six-lane divided highway[2] until its intersection with Maryland Route 49 (Braddock Road), where it becomes a four-lane non-divided highway.[2] Vocke Road provides access to Country Club Mall[1], as well as to several residential areas in LaVale. A short distance south of its intersection with Braddock Road, Vocke Road provides access to Interstate 68[1]. At Braddock Road, the name of Route 658 changes to Campground Road.[1] Campground Road continues northward until its terminus at U.S. Route 40 Alternate.[1]
