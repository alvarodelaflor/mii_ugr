Sirmione (racehorse)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

 This article does not cite any references or sources. (June 2008)
Please help improve this article by adding citations to reliable sources. Unverifiable material may be challenged and removed.

Sirmione is notable for winning the Mackinnon Stakes at 60-1 during the 2007 spring carnival. He went on to run 12th in the Melbourne Cup. He then went on to win the Australian Cup in March 2008 before running third in the BMW at Rosehill. He is trained by the Cups King Bart Cummings.
