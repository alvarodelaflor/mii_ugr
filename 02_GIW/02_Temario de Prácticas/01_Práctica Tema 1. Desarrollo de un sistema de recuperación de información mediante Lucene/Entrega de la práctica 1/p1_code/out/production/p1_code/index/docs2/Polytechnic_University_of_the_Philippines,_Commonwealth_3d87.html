Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Commonwealth Campus (or PUP - Commonwealth) is an extension of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, which caters to students residing in Quezon City, as well as the nearby cities and towns like Caloocan, Bulacan, and Rizal.

As a member of the PUP System, the University aims to provide quality education to poor but deserving students. As of 2007, the campus offers six undergraduate programs catering to the needs of the modern business world. Programs offered include:


  • Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurial Management
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration
  • Bachelor in Business Teacher Education
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Marketing Management
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Human Resources Development and Management
  • Master in Educational Management (distance learning mode)
  • Master in Public Administration
