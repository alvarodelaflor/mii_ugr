Soma Records
Founded 1991
Founder(s) Glenn Gibbons, Jim Moutune, Dave Clarke, Stuart McMillan, Orde Meikle
Genre(s) Techno, house
Country Scotland, UK
Website http://www.somarecords.com/

Soma Quality Recordings (more commonly known as Soma Records) is a Scottish techno and house label co-founded in 1991 by the electronic music duo Slam. It is currently known as the best dance music label in Scotland as well as one of the world's leading techno and house labels.[1] The label is known for releasing Daft Punk's first tracks from their 1997 album Homework.
