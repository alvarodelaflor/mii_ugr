Vitaliy Popkov

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Vitaliy Popkov (Виталий Попков, born June 16, 1983) is an Ukrainian professional racing cyclist.

[edit] Career highlights

  1. 2002: 2º in Moscou, Team Pursuit (RUS)
  2. 2002: 1º in European Championship, Track, Team Pursuit, U23, Buttgen (GER)
  3. 2003: 1º in European Championship, Track, Team Pursuit, U23, Moscou
  4. 2003: 1º in Cape Town, Team Pursuit (RSA)
  5. 2003: 1º in Stage 2 Tour de Hongrie, Bekescsaba (HUN)
  6. 2004: 1º in European Championship, Track, Team Pursuit, U23, Valencia
  7. 2004: 2º in Moscou, Team Pursuit (RUS)
  8. 2005: 1º in Moscou, Team Pursuit (RUS)
  9. 2005: 2º in European Championship, Track, Team Pursuit, U23, Fiorenzuola
  10. 2005: 3º in Moscou, Team Pursuit (RUS)
  11. 2006: 2º in Sydney, Team Pursuit (AUS)
  12. 2007: 1º in Los Angeles, Pursuit (USA)
  13. 2007: 1º in Los Angeles, Team Pursuit (USA)
  14. 2007: 2º in World Championship, Track, Team Pursuit, Elite, Palma de Mallorca (SPA)
  15. 2008: 3º in Los Angeles, Team Pursuit (USA)
