Weapons of Our Warfare is an album released by the Christian Speed/Thrash metal band Deliverance and is considered by many to be one of the best of their career. The album was originally released in 1990 on Intense Records and printings from that release are very rare and considered collectors items. The band rereleased the album in 1998 as a 2 disc set along with Deliverance on KMG records.
