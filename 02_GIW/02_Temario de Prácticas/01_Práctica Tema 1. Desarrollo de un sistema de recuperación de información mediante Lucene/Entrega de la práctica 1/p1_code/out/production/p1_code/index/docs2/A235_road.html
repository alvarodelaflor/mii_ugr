The A235 is a non-primary A road between Purley and Thornton Heath in the London Borough of Croydon.

The road stops halfway through, then starts again about two miles up on London Road. The London Road part of the road (near Thornton Heath Pond) is small but is a vital part of Croydon as many bus routes use it to reach Central London and Brixton. Mayday University Hospital is located on the road as well as the old Safari Cinema. Whitgift School is located on the Brighton Road stretch of the A235.
