Take Ya Home is the second single of Lil' Bow Wow's second album Doggy Bag. In the video he stands in his "Bow Wow Land" and when he sings the girls in the backgrounds began to dance and shaking their bootys. The song was used in Lil' Bow Wow's film Like Mike
