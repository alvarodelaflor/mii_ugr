Butch Hughes

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Butch Hughes played four years in the minors.

Hughes was the head coach at Merced Junior College from 1972 to 1981. He managed the GCL Cubs in 1993, the Reno Chukars in 1996-1998, the Yakima Bears in 2000, and the Great Falls Dodgers in 2001.

He has been a minor league pitching coach for the Milwaukee Brewers (1979-1980), Philadelphia Phillies (1982), San Francisco Giants (1983), Oakland A's (1985-1987), Chicago Cubs (1994), and Montreal Expos (1995). He was the pitching coach for the Tri-City Dust Devils in 2004, and currently (2006), he is the pitching coach of the Modesto Nuts.

Hughes also managed in Italy in 1984 and 1991.
